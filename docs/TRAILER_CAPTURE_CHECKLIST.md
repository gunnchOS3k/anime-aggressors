# Trailer Capture Checklist

Use when recording a public demo trailer or walkthrough.

---

## 30-second trailer structure

1. **0–3s** — Home screen logo + "Play Demo" pulse
2. **3–8s** — Fighter select flash (production badges)
3. **8–12s** — Skyline Arena stage preview
4. **12–18s** — Ember vs Rook combat — hit sparks, damage rising
5. **18–22s** — KO blast zone launch
6. **22–26s** — Juno speed clip OR Kaia aerial
7. **26–30s** — Title card + URL

## 60-second walkthrough structure

1. Home menu tour (Play Demo, Fighter/Stage Select, Training, Labs separation)
2. Full match: select → fight → results → rematch
3. Training mode: damage reset + dummy
4. Preview fighter teaser (1–2s) with "balance pending" visible
5. Controls overlay (H key)
6. End on results screen + site URL

---

## Browser recording settings

- **Resolution:** 1920×1080 or 1280×720
- **Frame rate:** 60 FPS preferred (game sim is 60 Hz)
- **Browser:** Chrome or Firefox, hardware acceleration on
- **Window:** fullscreen battle or clean 16:9 crop
- **Audio:** capture tab audio; note Web Audio needs click first
- **Hide:** debug panel (F1), hitboxes (F2), browser bookmarks bar

---

## What not to show

- Rollback debug, Edge-IO lab, prototype lab internals
- Console errors or failed Godot boot
- Misleading "finished game" cinematics — procedural art is intentional
- Career/story modes as if complete
- Mobile play (unsupported)

---

## Known rough edges — do not hide dishonestly

- Placeholder procedural audio
- Simple geometry VFX
- Preview fighter balance unevenness
- Occasional camera jitter on blast zone zoom
- Labs content is experimental

If a shot looks broken, capture a different angle or note in video description.
