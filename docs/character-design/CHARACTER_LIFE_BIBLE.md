## Shared presentation rules

1. No release-facing mannequin framing in selection or results.
2. Selection always shows silhouette, archetype line, and aura hint.
3. Victory/defeat never reuse combat idle unchanged.
4. Directional throws must show distinct body orientations.
5. Aura shape language differs by fighter (not only color).
