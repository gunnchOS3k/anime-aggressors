# Juno Spark — Production Spec

## 1. Fantasy
Rushdown lightning striker — fast commits, electric scarf trail.

## 2. Silhouette
Compact, yellow/black, bolt hair tufts, lightning sash.

## 3. Body type
Small / fast — lowest weight feel on roster.

## 4. Movement identity
Dash-heavy, double-jump extensions, afterimage on specials.

## 5. Difficulty
- Beginner: fast movement forgives mistakes
- Intermediate: hit-and-run confirms
- Advanced: volt cross into launch

## 6. Attack style
Quick jab flurry, volt cross side, rising spark, low volt sweep.

## 7. Special style
Lightning dash — invulnerable startup frames (tuned later).

## 8. Aura visual language
Electric halo and scarf flicker; super-ready arc between hands.

## 9. VFX sockets
- aura_core: chest
- afterimage anchor: back sash
- hands/feet: spark trails

## 11. Victory pose
Quick spark dash, afterimage, electric snap pose.

## 12. Defeat pose
Collapse with fading sparks.

## 13. Required assets
- `assets/blender/fighters/juno-spark/juno-spark.blend`
- `assets/exports/godot/fighters/juno-spark.glb`

## 14. Acceptance criteria
- [ ] Yellow/black silhouette distinct from Ember
- [ ] Dash shows limb motion + trail at feet
- [ ] Hit sparks yellow at contact socket
